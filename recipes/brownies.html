Description

This brownie recipe makes rich, fudgy brownies that turn out perfectly every time. You can make the best brownies ever! They always turn out!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • ⅔ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder
